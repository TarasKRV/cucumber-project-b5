Scenario 1: Searching for information 
Given I am on the Google search homepage
When I type in "COVID-19 updates"
Then I should see a list of relevant search results

Scenario 2: Using Google Maps 
Given I am on the Google Maps homepage
When I search for "nearest pharmacy"
Then I should see a list of nearby pharmacies and their locations on the map

Scenario 3: Using Google Translate 
Given I am on the Google Translate homepage
When I enter the word "hello" in English
Then I should see the translation in my chosen language, such as Spanish or French

Scenario 4: Using Google Images 
Given I am on the Google Images homepage
When I search for "cute puppies"
Then I should see a grid of adorable puppy pictures to browse through.
